WOMEN CYCLELATOR (WC)

  Menstruation and ovulation are two important cycles for women which sometimes affect women’s way of life. Getting through menstrual period is tough and may be painful for some women. Hence, a convenience and an effective system to facilitate women to track and manage their menstruation and ovulation is essential. Thus, the women can be physically and emotionally prepared to face these cycles in future. As a result, they can also organize their life better and happier. Women Cyclelator (WC) is a web-based application which is one of the solutions available.

    This web application helps women to organize their menstrual and ovulation cycles systematically in a synced calendar. WC also have functions such as period tracker, ovulation tracker and fertility tracker that ease women to schedule their menstrual date and clear about their fertility and ovulation date. Period reminder and fertility reminder are used to alert the women for their upcoming cycles. Keeping track of the menstruation can help the women discover their irregular menstruation immediately and manage this problem as soon as possible to prevent menstrual problems and improve reproductive health. Therefore, WC is a well development application for women.
